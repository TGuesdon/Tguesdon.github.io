Internship at Ochanomizu University in Tokyo, Japan | Spring 2018

This was a shared internship with Nathan Joubert and Gaëtan Basile, in which we developped a visualization software for CCG2Lambda. This allowed us to discover language theory and graph theory. We also did a website to show their results.

Technologies : Java, JavaFX, NodeJS, AWS
